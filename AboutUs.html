About Us

Sweetlicious Desserts is focused on providing beautiful and delicious cakes, cupcakes, cookies and desserts, while delivering high quality service and customer satisfaction. We will do everything we can to meet your expectations! From custom cakes, cookies and treats to table and event setup and planning, we strive to bring your sweet dreams to life!

We create cakes, that’s true. Each cake is a true labor of love, combining the perfect blend of inspiration, passion, and serious baking know-how. We love working with our clients who commemorate the joys, accomplishments, momentous occasions and wonders of life. We are there from the beginning, and we become part of the family, creating centerpieces for your most important celebrations: your wedding, your baby shower, your child’s first birthday…
